HOW DOES CHARGE WORK?
basically just advanced rock paper scissors.
each move has a charge cost, players start with 2 charges.
if player uses move that costs more charges than they have, POOF (they lose)

moves:

cost 0
- charge: +1 charge to player
- bang/spell/slash block: self-explanatory

cost 1
- bang

cost 3
- spell

cost 4
- slash
